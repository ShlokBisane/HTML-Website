Music Genres

Genre Description Notable Artists Image
Phonk A subgenre of hip hop that blends elements of Memphis rap, often featuring lo-fi production and samples from 90s hip hop. DJ Smokey, $uicideboy$, Bones
Pop A genre that is designed to appeal to a general audience, often featuring catchy melodies and simple lyrics. Michael Jackson, Madonna, Taylor Swift
Rap A musical form of vocal delivery that incorporates rhyme, rhythmic speech, and street vernacular. Eminem, Jay-Z, Nas
Hip Hop A genre that includes rhythmic vocal style (rapping) and DJing, often addressing social issues and personal experiences. Tupac Shakur, The Notorious B.I.G., Kendrick Lamar
