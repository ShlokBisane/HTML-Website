Culture

Mughal Architecture
Agra is renowned for its stunning Mughal architecture, with intricate designs and grand structures that reflect a rich historical legacy.
Festivals
The city celebrates various festivals, including Diwali and Eid, showcasing its diverse cultural heritage and traditions.
Traditional Crafts
Agra is famous for its marble inlay work and leather goods, reflecting the artistry and craftsmanship of local artisans.
Music and Dance
The city has a vibrant cultural scene with classical music and dance performances that celebrate its rich heritage.

Places

Taj Mahal
A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love and architectural brilliance.
Agra Fort
A majestic fort that showcases the grandeur of Mughal architecture and offers stunning views of the Taj Mahal.
Fatehpur Sikri
A former Mughal capital, this city is known for its beautiful palaces and mosques, reflecting the glory of the Mughal Empire.
Mehtab Bagh
A beautiful garden located across the Yamuna River, offering a stunning view of the Taj Mahal, especially at sunset.

Food

Mughlai Cuisine
Agra is famous for its rich Mughlai cuisine, featuring dishes like biryani, kebabs, and rich curries that tantalize the taste buds.
Petha
A sweet delicacy made from ash gourd, Petha is a must-try when visiting Agra, available in various flavors.
Chaat
A popular street food, Chaat in Agra is known for its tangy and spicy flavors, offering a delightful snack experience.
Lassi
A refreshing yogurt-based drink, Lassi is a perfect way to cool down while exploring the bustling streets of Agra.
