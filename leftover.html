AGRA

Agra, home to the world-famous Taj Mahal, is a city steeped in Mughal history and architectural grandeur. The Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri are UNESCO World Heritage sites that showcase the city's rich heritage. Agra's vibrant bazaars offer exquisite handicrafts and traditional sweets. The city's blend of history, culture, and beauty makes it a must-visit destination.

JAISALMER

Jaisalmer, known as the Golden City, is a stunning desert town in Rajasthan. It's famous for its magnificent sandstone fort, charming havelis, and vibrant bazaars. The Thar Desert provides a picturesque backdrop for camel safaris and cultural performances. Jaisalmer's rich heritage and warm hospitality make it a captivating destination.
